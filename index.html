Devops Engineer

Within a development team, there are developers and the operations team. Both parties have different goals; developers want to improve the software by releasing new features, whereas the operations team wants to keep the system stable and reliable. DevOps engineers are responsible for implementing tools, values, and policies to integrate both goals.
